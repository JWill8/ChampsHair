Who We Are

Champs Natural Living & Locking is a private studio that specializes in the establishment and maintenance of Sisterlocks and Brotherlocks, a trademark technique for locking naturally coiled/textured hair. Sisterlocks and Brotherlocks is not a fad, or a short-term hairdo. It is a natural hair management system that creates tiny/small/medium locks that allows those with tightly textured hair to take advantage of a wide range of todayâ€™s hairstyles without having to alter the natural texture of their hair or add any products or heat. You can expect a professional environment that provides individual attention to your Sisterlocks and Brotherlocks needs.

A few highlights of what we offer:

  • Time efficient retightening appointments
  • Private/personal attention rather than the open open/busy environment of a typical salon
  • Retail of official Sisterlocks branded products
  • Official instructor for self-retightening classes and other specialty hair care classes
  • Wifi usage available

If you're looking for a hairstyle that is easy to maintain, looks great and saves you time and money, give us a try!

About Me

Hello! My name is Gina Championsmith and I'm a certified Sisterlocks™ Brand Amabassador and Constultant. 20 years ago, a bad perm forced me to completely shave my head. After that, I wore natural styles for fear of another bad experience with harsh chemicals until one day, I saw someone with a head of gorgeous Sisterlocks. Curious, I spent that night researching everything I could find about this new (to me) styling technique. 3 weeks later, I had a head full of baby Sisterlocks and after 10 years I haven't looked back. I have since become Sisterlocks™ Brand Ambassador, Consultant and for the last seven years I have helped bring this same wonderful hairstyle to others. I started building my business in California and have since moved to Seattle's south end and. Today I am proud to say that I've helped over 100 clients enjoy the same hair that I do.
